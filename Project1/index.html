The Health and Wealth of Nations

Life Expectancy & Economy (GDP per capita)


This visualization shows how Life Expectancy (in years), Ecomony (GDP per Capita) and Population have changed in different regions over the past two centuries. It was inspired by a famouse visualization of Hans Rosling. Plotting Life Expectancy against Income for every country since 1810, Hans showed how the world we live in is radically different from the world most of us imagine to be: Gapminder.
Data source: Our World in Data.
